## 1. Overview
This router acts as an edge router to provide an internet connection to the entire network via its WAN port. 
From LAN port 1, a cable runs through a patch panel mounted on a switch rack. The corresponding patch panel port on the other side of the lab connects directly to the wireless access point
This was done as there was no WAN port available at the AP's physical location.
